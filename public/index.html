Who We Are

A spin-off start up firm from ETH Zurich (Swizerland) and Kyoto University (Japan). We solve real world problems with practical experiences and state-of-the-art innovations in engineering and science

Technical Due Diligence

We conduct Due Diligence studies on building systems (office, retail, commercial and shopping malls), Infrastructure systems (Bridges, Roads, Water Utilities), and industrial sites (logistic centers, warehouses, factories) using innovative digital tools and by experienced engineers to help Capital Investors arriving at optimal Merging and Acquisition (M&A) decisions

Asset Management

We assist organizations to develop long term asset management blueprints and programs for sustainable growth. We build models to determine optimal preventive intervention strategies on assets using reliability based approach

Data Analytics and Modelling

We detect trends and identify bottlenecks of engineering systems using data science and advise Clients on effective solutions

Research & Education

In close collaboration with ETH Zurich, Kyoto University, and local universities, we are contributing to training & education for a next generation of reliability engineers in the region
